Social network posts

Ecostyle Appared

Design of 2 banners, 6 posts, and 2 stories for Instagram.

Role

Graphic Designer

Tools

  • Figma
  • Illustrator

Time

3 days

Description

Simulated project designing a set of graphic posts for social networks, designing 6 posts (carousel and single post), 2 banners, and 2 stories for Instagram.

About the client

To start designing this project, I generated a simulated client. Ecostyle Appared is an innovative brand dedicated to elaborate ecological and sustainable clothes and accessories. They use recycled materials, ethical manufacturing processes, and promote conscious fashion to reduce environmental impact. Their goal is to create elegant clothes that respect both personal style and the planet.

The main goal of this social media campaign is to promote responsible consumption and to promote EcoStyle Apparel's sustainable clothing.

Moodboard

This step consisted of searching for ideas based on the brand style: Boho-chic.

Style guide

We defined and generated the color palette based on boho-chic style, which combined green, soft, and earthy tones, evoking the minimalism and images with superimposed elements.

The font used is Lora for titles and Roboto for text.

Mockups

See Mockups in Figma

The requirement of all posts is to have the brand logo in a visible place, images with the brand clothes in natural places, using the brand colors, and applying the text provided by the company with a call to action. The brief also indicated what each slide of the post carousel would contain.
