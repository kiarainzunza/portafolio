Promotional Reels

Design of promotional reels for social media.

Role

Graphic designer

Tools

  • Illustrator
  • After Effects

Time

3 weeks

Description

A series of motion graphics projects designed as practice exercises using After Effects as a main tool.

Each graphic piece is designed to be used as an advertisement for social media.

PCFactory

Practice reel in After Effects for a fictional PCFactory Gamer Day campaign.

In this exercise, I focus on generating text and image movement accompanied by transitions, which could be used to simulate discount promotions in the technology sector.

I started designing every screen of the reel, considering the transitions and movement to plan the design in detail.

Design

Finally, I transferred it to Illustrator and then replicated it in After Effects.

Frutillita

Practice reel in After Effects for a fictional frutillita campaign.

In this case, I designed the general aesthetic considering the colors and typography because I had a previous general idea of the transitions and order of the project.

Design

To continue the project, I designed all the screens in Illustrator, applying the graphic elements, and then replicated and animated the project in After Effects.

Important: Click on each image to see it in full.

Beanywood Café

Practice reel in After Effects for a fictional campaign of Beanywood Café, focused on maintaining the graphic style of the brand and presenting the products.

Design

After designing the graphic elements, I designed all the pages separately and then replicated them in After Effects and animated them.
